Who we are?

A service or after-sales service defines an assistance and support service provided by a brand for anyone who has purchased or consumed its products. The purpose of after-sales service is to ensure that consumers are satisfied with their purchase or order, and to provide assistance in the event of a problem or breakdown. Indeed, the purchase of a new product always comes with the risk that it does not work properly or does not quite meet our expectations. It is in these cases that it is imperative to contact the after-sales services, which are managed by customer and technical advisors who can solve any type of problem and complaint.
